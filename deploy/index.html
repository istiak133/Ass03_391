Help & Guide

Everything you need to know about booking an appointment.

How do I book an appointment? ▾
1. Go to "Book Appointment" from the navigation bar.
2. Fill in your name, address, phone number, car license and engine number.
3. Select your preferred appointment date.
4. Click on a mechanic to see their available slots.
5. Select an available mechanic (not marked as FULL).
6. Click "Review Booking" to see a summary.
7. Click "Confirm Booking" to finalize.
What if my preferred mechanic is fully booked? ▾
If a mechanic has no available slots for your selected date, their card will show "ALL SLOTS BOOKED" and the next available date will be displayed. You can either choose a different mechanic with available slots, or change your appointment date.
Can I book multiple appointments on the same day? ▾
Yes. You can book appointments with different mechanics on the same date, for example if you have multiple cars that need servicing. However, you cannot book the same car with the same mechanic on the same date twice.
How do I view or cancel my appointments? ▾
Go to "My Appointments" from the navigation bar. Enter the phone number you used during booking and click "Search". All your appointments will be listed with their status. Active appointments will have a "Cancel" button.
What are the form field requirements? ▾
Name: Cannot be empty.
Address: Cannot be empty.
Phone: Must contain only digits (numbers).
Car License Number: Cannot be empty.
Car Engine Number: Must contain only digits.
Date: Must be a today or future date (no past dates).
Mechanic: Must select one with available slots.
What do the appointment statuses mean? ▾
Active: Your appointment is confirmed and scheduled.
Completed: The service has been done.
Cancelled: The appointment was cancelled by you or the admin.
